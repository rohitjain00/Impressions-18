About The Imitation Game

This event is for all the artists out there who want to try something new, something they have never tried before, so pack your bags with everyday items like bed sheets, towels etc. and hit floor with all you have got

In this event, every team would be given a picture to imitate it on the ground with help of everyday things. The best they match the picture more the chance to win.

  • Every team cotains maximum 4 members.
  • Picture must be drawn on an area of 1.5m approx.
  • Every team must bring there own material.
  • Using of colors in any form is prohibitted.
  • Random picture would be provided on the spot
