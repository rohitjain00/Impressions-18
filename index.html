About The Imitation Game

This event is for all the artists out there who want to try something new, something they have never tried before, so pack your bags with everyday items like bed sheets, towels etc. and hit floor with all you have got

In this event, every team would be given a picture to imitate on the ground with the help of everyday things. The best they match the more the chance to win.

  • Every team contains maximum 4 members.
  • Picture must be drawn on an area of 1.5m2 approx.
  • Every team must bring their own material.
  • Using of colors in any form is prohibited.
  • Random picture would be provided on the spot
Be present outside old LRC on Feb, 24th @ 11AM
